# DPIA Template

Use this template when a feature or workflow may present elevated privacy risk.

## Trigger Check

Consider a DPIA when the change involves:

- large-scale personal data processing
- special category or highly sensitive data
- systematic monitoring or profiling
- automated decision-making with significant impact
- cross-border transfer with elevated risk
- new combinations of data that materially increase privacy exposure

## Template

- initiative or feature
- description of processing
- purpose and necessity
- data categories
- data subjects
- systems and vendors involved
- risks to individuals
- mitigations and controls
- residual risk
- decision and approver
