# Root Cause Policy

## Principle

Do not stop at the visible symptom by default. Investigate the layer that actually allowed the problem to occur.

## Required Questions

- What failed first?
- Is the issue local or systemic?
- Did requirements, architecture, data shape, UX, observability, or tests contribute?
- What change prevents recurrence?
- Is a workaround being mistaken for a durable solution?

## Workaround Rule

Emergency mitigations are allowed, but they must be labeled temporary and paired with a structural remediation path.
