# Vendor Intake Checklist

Use this checklist before adopting a new external dependency, SaaS, infrastructure provider, or integration vendor.

## Checklist

- business purpose is clear
- data categories handled are known
- security review is completed
- privacy and subprocessor impact is known
- residency and transfer implications are known
- contract or terms review is completed
- exit strategy is known
- cost and rate-limit assumptions are known
