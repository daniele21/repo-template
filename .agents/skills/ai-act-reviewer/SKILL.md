# AI Act Reviewer

Use this skill when AI is proposed, introduced, or materially changed.

## Inputs

- project brief
- brainstorming doc
- solution strategy
- delivery plan
- architecture context
- AI-related requirements or provider choices

## Workflow

1. Check whether AI is actually part of the product or only being considered.
2. Describe the role AI plays and whether it touches sensitive or regulated workflows.
3. Surface human-oversight, provider-governance, logging, eval, and output-control needs.
4. Flag when AI Act or broader AI governance concerns should be documented.
5. Avoid claiming legal certainty and escalate legal interpretation when needed.

## Output

- whether AI is in scope
- AI role in the product
- main governance concerns
- AI Act applicability signals
- human oversight needs
- logging, eval, and output-control needs
- provider / model governance considerations
- documentation to create or update
- residual risks

## Review Questions

- Is AI actually necessary for the intended workflow?
- Could model outputs affect rights, access, eligibility, or sensitive outcomes?
- What oversight and documentation are required before shipping?
